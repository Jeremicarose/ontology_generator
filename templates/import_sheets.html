How It Works

This tool imports data from a Google Sheet to generate your ontology. The spreadsheet must contain the following worksheets:

  • taxonomic_hierarchy
  • biomes
  • ecoregions
  • countries
  • object_properties
  • data_properties

Sharing Your Spreadsheet

Make sure your Google Sheet is shared with our service account:

service-account@project-id.iam.gserviceaccount.com

Give it at least "Viewer" permissions to allow reading the data.

Tips

  • Each worksheet should have the correct column names
  • Data should match the expected format from CSV files
  • Keep the data well-structured for best results
